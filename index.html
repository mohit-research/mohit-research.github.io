Mohit Agrawal

A hearty welcome! I am Mohit Agrawal — a Senior Lead Engineer at Qualcomm AI Software, where I work on AI systems at scale, and am concurrently pursuing a PhD at IIT Kharagpur under the supervision of Dr. Pawan Goyal. My industry and research work are deeply intertwined — one shapes the other.

My research sits at the intersection of Multimodal AI and Education — building intelligent systems that can listen, speak, and meaningfully evaluate human learning. I work on Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Text-to-Speech (TTS), and the evaluation of multimodal AI systems. A core belief driving my work is that powerful AI should not be locked behind expensive cloud infrastructure, which is why I am actively exploring Small Language Models (SLMs) and on-device AI to bring these capabilities directly to edge devices, making quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere — even without an internet connection.

Before my current role, I built my engineering foundations across some exciting places — Teradata R&D Labs, IBM ISDL, Blaize, and IIIT Delhi, where I worked as a Research Assistant under Prof. Rajiv Ratn Shah. These experiences shaped how I think about building AI that works not just in research papers, but in real-world, resource-constrained systems.

I hold a Master's in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (2016), advised by Dr. R. Leela Velusamy for my thesis titled "Spam Detection in Social Networking Sites Using Unsupervised Learning and Dimensionality Reduction." I completed my Bachelor's in CSE from Government College of Engineering, Bhawanipatna (2013).

Outside of research, I enjoy teaching, writing, and travelling — three things that keep reminding me why building AI for education matters. If you are working on multimodal AI, educational technology, or edge AI, I would love to connect!
