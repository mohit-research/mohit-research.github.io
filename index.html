Mohit Agrawal  

A hearty Welcome! I am Mohit Agrawal, a Senior Lead Engineer at Qualcomm. I am also a PhD student at IIT Kharagpur, where I work with Prof. Pawan Goyal in the area of Multimodal Learning applied to Education. Prior to this, I spent a wonderful time as a Senior Software Engineer at Teradata R&D Labs, working in the area of distributed machine learning. I also worked as a research assistant at IIIT Delhi, where I was advised by Prof. Rajiv Ratn Shah and Prof. Rajesh Kumar.

I received my Master's and Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli and Government College of Engineering, Bhawanipatna in 2016 and 2013, respectively. During my master's I was advised by Prof. R. Leela Velusamy for my thesis titled "Spam Detection in Social Networking Sites Using Unsupervised Learning and Dimensionality Reduction".

My research area lies in the area of AI for Education. Currently, my research focuses on the intersection of Low Resource Languages, Planning/Reasoning, Speech Processing, and NLP.

Apart from my academic interests, I like to teach, write stuff, and travel. If you are interested in my research or have any use cases that you want to share, feel free to contact me!
