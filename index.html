Mohit Agrawal  

Currently, I am working as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Qualcomm. I am also a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering department at IIIT Delhi, advised by Prof. Rajiv Ratn Shah and Prof. Rajesh Kumar . Prior to this, I spent a wonderful time as a Senior Software Engineer at Teradata R&D Labs , working in the area of distributed machine learning.

I received my M.S./M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering and B.S./B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli and Government College of Engineering, Bhawanipatna in 2016 and 2013, respectively. During my master's I was advised by Prof. R. Leela Velusamy for my thesis titled "Spam Detection in Social Networking Sites Using Unsupervised Learning and Dimensionality Reduction".

My research area lies in Biometrics, Security, Social Network Analysis, and Machine/Deep Learning. Currently, my research focuses on Adversarial Robustness. I am exploring novel adversarially robust methods for biometrics research problems, such as Authentication and Recognition. I also work on Domain Adaptation, Fairness, and resource constrained AI.

Apart from my academic interests, I like to teach, write stuff, and travel. If you are interested in my research or have any use cases that you want to share, feel free to contact me!
